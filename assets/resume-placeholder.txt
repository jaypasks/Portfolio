## Resume Tips:

1. **Format**: Use PDF format for best compatibility
2. **File Size**: Keep under 2MB for fast downloads
3. **Content**: Include contact info, experience, skills, education
4. **Design**: Keep it clean and professional
5. **Keywords**: Include relevant technical keywords
6. **Length**: 1-2 pages is typically ideal

## Tools for Creating Resumes:

- **Online**: Canva, Resume.io, Zety
- **Software**: Microsoft Word, Google Docs, LaTeX
- **Design**: Adobe InDesign, Figma
